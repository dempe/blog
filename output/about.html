    About

    Hello! I'm Chris Dempewolf (dim-pee-wolf), software engineer and former ESL teacher.

    I was born in the Fort Smith, Arkansas, USA. I lived in Seattle for 2 years and abroad in Mexico, Japan, and Germany. I taught English for a couple of years and have worked as a software engineer for 7 years.

    I created this blog for two reasons:

    1. To share what I learn
    2. To help me learn

    I don't really know what I know until I write about it, so this blog is meant to serve as my light in the dark. I write about computation, biology, cooking, and everything else that piques my interest. View an archive of articles I've written here.

    Thanks for stopping by!
